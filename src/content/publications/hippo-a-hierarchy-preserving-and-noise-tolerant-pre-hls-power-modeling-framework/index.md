## Abstract

HIPPO provides pre-HLS power modeling for FPGA accelerators while preserving program hierarchy and tolerating noisy training labels. The framework aims to estimate power early in the HLS design process, helping designers evaluate power-performance tradeoffs before costly synthesis and implementation steps.
