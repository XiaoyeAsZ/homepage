## Abstract

StreamingGS is an algorithm-architecture co-design for real-time 3D Gaussian Splatting on resource-constrained devices. It reformulates rendering around voxel-based streaming and memory-centric processing to reduce DRAM traffic, improve pipelining, and accelerate Gaussian Splatting with better energy efficiency.
