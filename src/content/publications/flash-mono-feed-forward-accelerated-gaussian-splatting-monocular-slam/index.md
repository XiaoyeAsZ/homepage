## Abstract

Flash-Mono accelerates monocular Gaussian Splatting SLAM with a feed-forward frontend that predicts Gaussian attributes from multi-frame context. It combines recurrent visual feature aggregation, 2D Gaussian Splatting mapping, and hidden-state-based loop closure to improve runtime, geometry consistency, and reconstruction quality.
