## Abstract

DHAP targets efficient OLAP execution in systems where compute, memory, and accelerators are increasingly disaggregated and heterogeneous. The work focuses on coordinating data placement, execution scheduling, and heterogeneous resources so analytical queries can benefit from disaggregation without being dominated by data-movement overhead.
