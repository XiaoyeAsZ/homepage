## Abstract

This paper studies how to map heterogeneous DNN models onto adaptive multi-accelerator systems. It models accelerator selection, model partitioning, and communication-aware mapping together so that diverse DNN workloads can exploit parallelism across available accelerator resources while reducing execution latency.
